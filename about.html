About me

Food is my passion! I believe that the work and thought involved in successfully executing on an idea is one of the best feelings in the world; and for me, that is most easily achieved in the kitchen (or outside at the grill). 10 years ago, I traveled to Colombia for the first time with my soon to be wife and had my first ever colombian food experience. Being from the pacific northwest, Colombians and their food is not something that is easy to find. I was blown away by the delicious yet seemingly simple ingredient profile. To me, learning and preparing authentic colombian dishes (and my gringo renditions of them) is akin to mastering the comfort food that flows through my (questionable) southern roots. Buen provecho!
-Lucas Metz.
